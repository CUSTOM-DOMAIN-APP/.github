# Security policy

Custom Domain operates public trust infrastructure (DNS automation and TLS issuance for customer domains), so we take reports seriously.

- **Report a vulnerability:** email **legal@customdomain.ai** with details and reproduction steps. We acknowledge within 2 business days.
- **Scope:** the customdomain.ai product and APIs, the hosted MCP server (mcp.customdomain.ai), the embeddable widget, and the repositories in this organization.
- **Our posture:** security practices, compliance frameworks (SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR), policies, and sub-processors are published on our **[Trust Center](https://trust.customdomain.ai)**.

Please do not open public issues for security reports.
