# Contributing

Thanks for helping improve the Custom Domain guides and documentation.

- **Docs** live in [CUSTOM-DOMAIN-APP/docs](https://github.com/CUSTOM-DOMAIN-APP/docs) (`content/`). Merged changes sync to the product site automatically.
- **Guide repos** (`connect-domain-for-*`) welcome corrections, additional provider notes, and clearer examples.
- **awesome-custom-domains** accepts tools directly relevant to connecting customer-owned domains to platforms: one line per tool, alphabetical within its section, factual descriptions.

House style: plain language, short paragraphs, tables where they clarify, no em dashes or en dashes, American English, real DNS/TLS facts only (no invented numbers). Typo PRs are always welcome.
